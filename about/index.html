About PandaStake

PandaStake is a fully open-source, testnet-only ETH staking platform launched in June 2025 on the Sepolia network. Our primary goal is education—allowing anyone to practice staking in a safe environment using Sepolia testnet ETH. Users learn DeFi and staking mechanics without risking real funds.

The project was initiated by a young computer science graduate, a passionate blockchain enthusiast who chooses to remain anonymous. He created PandaStake driven by a unique vision for the future of staking and token utility. He believes that a well-designed token can deliver real, tangible value to investors. Consequently, we are developing our own “PANDA” token, which in the future will offer use cases extending well beyond simple staking.

Team & Mission

Initially conceived solo, the project quickly attracted several contributors with expertise in smart contract development and UI/UX design. Today, we have a small but rapidly growing team, each member bringing specialized skills (backend, frontend, smart contract security, marketing). Over time, we plan to expand our technical and marketing staff to meet the challenges of transforming this testnet version into a fully operational DeFi ecosystem.

Our mission is to demonstrate how Ethereum staking works (on the Sepolia testnet) and prepare users for real DeFi on the mainnet (targeting Polygon). We aim to educate everyone—from complete beginners unfamiliar with MetaMask to advanced users seeking to understand reward calculation and smart contract security.

What We Offer Today

  • Testnet ETH Staking
    Users can connect their MetaMask wallet and safely stake Sepolia test ETH. This process operates in “testnet only” mode: the tokens you stake are not real Ether, so you incur no risk of financial loss.
  • Presale Signup Form
    We invite visitors to leave their email address so they can be the first to know when the PANDA token presale goes live. Emails are stored securely, and all notifications are sent via our partner email service, Migadu, guaranteeing data safety.
  • Token Price Simulator
    The “Token Price Simulator” slider lets you see how market capitalization would change at various hypothetical PANDA token prices—from $0.05 up to $10. Assuming a total supply of 2,000,000 PANDA, this illustrates potential investor returns.
  • Neon-Themed Interface & Responsive Design
    Our “cyber-neon” style (dark background #1e1e1e, neon green accents #2ecc71, subtle glow effects) is fully responsive for desktop, tablet, and mobile.
  • Open Source & Full Documentation
    All smart contracts (StakingETH.sol) and deployment scripts (deploy.js), as well as Hardhat configuration, are publicly available in our GitHub repository:
    github.com/PandaStake
    Legal files (Disclaimer, Terms of Service, Privacy Policy, Contact) can be found under “Docs” on our website.

Future Roadmap

PANDA Token Launch
When market conditions are favorable (targeting Polygon for lower gas fees), we will issue our PANDA ERC-20 token. Its primary utility will revolve around staking and participation in exclusive reward programs.

PANDA Staking & Swap Mechanisms
We will expand our ecosystem with smart contracts enabling:
– Staking PANDA tokens (users can stake PANDA to earn additional rewards)
– A simple ETH ↔ PANDA swap mechanism (initially simulated, then a full DEX protocol on Polygon)

Additional Tokens & Partnerships
Depending on demand and collaborations, we plan to add support for staking other partner tokens and yield farming features.

Team Expansion & Industry Partnerships
We aim to recruit blockchain enthusiasts and seasoned developers to continuously enhance the platform. We also plan collaborations with smart contract auditors (to increase credibility) and marketing partners (crypto influencers).
